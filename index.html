Teams

  • Teams of 2–3 people.
  • Register as a team or solo — sign up individually and we'll help match you.
  • Team up across departments, within your team, or with people you don't usually work with.
  • No team yet? No problem — sign up anyway.
  • We're aiming for 5–10 teams max.

The day

  • The whole event takes around 4 hours — teamwork, prototyping, and final pitches.
  • No prep needed in advance, beyond thinking up ideas.
  • Joining from the Finland office? Food and drinks are on us.
  • The final hour is for presentations to the jury.
